Instructions

  • Step One: Swiping a morph rate that you want for your two images.

  • Step Two: Click on the "Choose File" bottom, and select a front facial image with .jpg or .jpeg file extension.

  • Step Three: Click on another "Choose File" bottom, and select a front facial image with .jpg or .jpeg file extension.

  • Step Four: Click on the ""Morph"" bottom, and a lovely Morphed image will show up in next page.

  • Step Five: If you like your morphed image, you could click on the "Download The Morphed Image" bottom to download your lovely image.
